# Card artwork attribution

All 28 card illustrations are local copies of the pinned
[OpenMoji 17.0 color set](https://openmoji.org/) at 618×618 PNG resolution.
OpenMoji is designed by the OpenMoji contributors at HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd and
licensed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/).
Use of the artwork does not imply that OpenMoji endorses TopThis.

The downloaded image bytes are unmodified; only their local filenames differ.
The complete per-card title, codepoint, pinned source URL, local filename,
project/creator credit, license and modification status are recorded in
`content/card-art.sources.json`. Any adaptation of these graphics must be
distributed under CC BY-SA 4.0 with appropriate credit and an indication of the
changes.

Run `pnpm.cmd art:fetch` to reproduce the downloads and `pnpm.cmd art:check` to
verify runtime-catalog/manifest/file parity, distinct sources, required
attribution, PNG signatures and dimensions.
